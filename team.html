Badminton Team (Spring Semester)

Badminton is a spring sport at SRV, which means that it runs from around February to May. You are expected to have a 2.0 GPA or higher and fill out an athletic clearance form (which can be found on the home page) in order to participate on the team. Being on the team also gives you PE credit. (only for sophomores, juniors, and seniors)
