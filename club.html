Badminton Club

The purpose of the badminton club at SRV is to provide an opportunity to those who want extra time to play, train, or improve their fitness for badminton and encourage their social engagement. The club aims to provide exposure to the community on the sport of badminton. We also host 1-2 school-wide badminton tournaments (open to all students and staff) to shed light on the sport of badminton.

When We Meet

We meet every first and third Tuesday in the Aux Gym during lunch. We'd love to see you there.
