Badminton Basics

Frequently Asked Questions

No experience is required to join the club or even come to practice or training sessions. Anyone is welcome!

For club related activities, there are spare rackets however it is recommended that you bring your own if you can. For team related activities, you must bring your own racket. For both team and club related activities, birdies and nets will be provided.

The season takes place in second semester and runs from with tryouts in February, practice starting in March and the season ending in mid-May.

The only form you have to fill out is an athletic clearance form to ensure that you're healthy enough to play (only required for team).

Tryouts are held for one week during February. Make sure to also complete the athletic clearance form to ensure that we can let you on the team. More information to come.
